About

The Story Behind LUX INVESTING

  • The Concept

    The inspiration came when the founder noticed something interesting while exploring the resale market. Certain designer pieces weren’t just holding their value… they were increasing over time.

  • Founding LUX

    What started as curiosity quickly turned into research. By comparing original retail prices to resale values, a pattern emerged: some luxury goods were performing similarly to traditional investments.

  • Modern Luxury Investing

    What started as a small idea has grown into a platform focused on, analyzing luxury resale trends, highlighting high-performing assets, and educating a new generation of investors because in today’s world, your wardrobe can be your portfolio!

  • Our Mission

    Making luxury investment accessible & strategic!

    LUX INVESTING helps users:

    • track value growth of luxury items
    • understand which pieces appreciate over time
    • view fashion through a financial lens

LUX INVESTING – Key Market Stats

Data-Driven Insights into Luxury Asset Growth

Luxury handbags have shown steady long-term growth, averaging an 11.8% annual return over the past decade, driven by limited availability, iconic design, and strong global demand.

The luxury resale market, valued at $38.2 billion, is expanding as consumers favor sustainable and investment-driven shopping, creating more opportunities to buy, sell, and profit from high-end goods.

Handbags are the top-performing luxury asset, excelling in value retention and appreciation. Iconic designs and limited releases fuel strong resale demand, making them a prime focus for luxury investors.
